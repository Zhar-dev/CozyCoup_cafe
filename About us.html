Our History

Cozy Cup Cafe was founded by Al-nizar Wahab and Christian Clavite, who shared a passion for great coffee and a vision for creating a welcoming gathering spot for locals. What started as a humble storefront has blossomed into a beloved fixture of the neighborhood, thanks to the support of our loyal customers and dedicated team. Over the years, we've grown and evolved, but our commitment to quality and community remains unchanged. From our carefully crafted espresso drinks to our freshly baked pastries, every item on our menu is made with love and care. As we look to the future, we're excited to continue serving our community and spreading joy, one cup at a time. Whether you're a regular or a first-time visitor, we can't wait to welcome you to the Cozy Cup family!

Our Mission

At Cozy Cup Cafe, our mission is to provide a warm and inviting atmosphere where friends, families, and colleagues can gather to enjoy exceptional coffee, delicious food, and genuine hospitality. We strive to source the finest quality coffee beans from sustainable and ethical suppliers, ensuring every cup served is not only delicious but also supports communities and the environment. With a commitment to excellence, we aim to exceed our customers' expectations by consistently delivering exceptional service, fostering a sense of community, and creating memorable experiences that keep them coming back. At Cozy Cup Cafe, we believe that every cup tells a story, and we're dedicated to being a part of yours.
